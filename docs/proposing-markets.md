# Proposing Markets

Have a question? How about an idea? On Trueo, you can turn your questions into live, tradable prediction markets.

Proposing a market is permissionless and takes only a few steps.

## Step 1 — Select a model

Start by selecting the model you wish to use to structure your market.

![Model selection dropdown showing General, Crypto, Grok, and Template options](/img/proposing-step1-model.png)

## Step 2 — Describe your market

Input your question or idea into the prompt box. Feel free to include web links, additional context, or specific criteria.

Or keep it simple and let our AI agent do the rest.

![Create a Prediction Market Proposal screen with an idea prompt box](/img/proposing-step2-describe.jpg)

## Step 3 — Review the rules

Review the rules to ensure that you approve of the criteria. If you're satisfied, click **Accept**.

During this step you may edit the rules to fit your expectations. Upon edit, an AI evaluator will automatically review your changes and grade the overall structure of the contract.

![AI-generated market Question and Rules shown for review](/img/proposing-step3-rules.jpg)

> **Please note:** all markets must meet our integrity standards to be eligible for resolution. Markets that violate these standards are not eligible for YES/NO resolutions — they will instead be cancelled in the event that they graduate. For a full list of the guidelines, see the [Market Integrity Standards](./market-integrity-standards.md).

## Step 4 — Add a category and cover image (optional)

Select a category and provide a cover image to improve visibility on the proposal page.

![Set category and appearance step with image upload and category selector](/img/proposing-step4-appearance.jpg)

## Step 5 — Preview and propose

Preview your market, then click **Propose Market** if you're satisfied.

Your proposal is now ready to enter the [funding phase](./proposal-funding.md).

![Market proposed confirmation dialog with a View Proposal button](/img/proposing-step5-proposed.jpg)
